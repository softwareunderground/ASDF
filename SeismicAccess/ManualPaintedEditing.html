Painted editing

This is just an idea for DIY seismic data creation. When testing stuff you often need to create specific data and how can you make it? Well, for example using OpendTect's 'Simple File' format in combination with awk or perl will get you there.

For less nerdy people, a tool to interactively modify existing seismics may just be what they want. Managers may not like it that much (create your own nice hydrocarbons trap). But still.
